Sales. Forecasting Dashboard

1. Summary

This project looks at retail sales data to get business insights and see how well the company is doing. I made a dashboard with Power BI to show important numbers analyze sales over time and predict short-term sales to help make good decisions.

2. Tools and Technologies

- Power BI: Used for making the dashboard and predicting sales

- Microsoft Excel: Used for cleaning and preparing the data

3. Main Features

- Tracking numbers like:

Total Sales

Total Profit

Quantity Sold

Average Delivery Time

- Looking at sales performance by:

Region

Category and Sub-category

Customer Segment

Payment Method

Shipping Method

- Comparing sales and profit over months and years

- Predicting sales for the next 15 days with a range of possible values

5. Business Insights

The dashboard helps stakeholders:

- Find out which categories and regions are doing

- Track revenue and profit over months

- See how well shipping and delivery are working

- Recognize patterns in sales, over time

- Use predictions to plan short-term sales

6. Results

The dashboard gives a view of business performance letting management easily track important numbers find opportunities and make good short-term decisions based on sales data and predictions.
